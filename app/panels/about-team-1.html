Greg Heuss

Chief Executive Officer

Greg has 20 years of start-up leadership. He started his career in the music and entertainment industry before becoming one of the early members of the original Amazon.com marketing team. He has taken companies public, merged with fortune 100 corpor­ations and has a vast background in mobile based products, SAAS and subscription revenue models, and has helped build some of the biggest brands in the world.

Two Favorite Hobbies:

A hobby of mine that is turning into a business is wine-making - I'm on my fourth vintage and starting a winery with a few friends. My most fulfilling hobby is volunteering at Little Bit Therapeutic Riding - helping children with disabilities overcome fears and obstacles via the help of fantastic four legged "therapists" - horses.
