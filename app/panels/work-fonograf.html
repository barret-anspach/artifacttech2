Fonograf

We are music fans. We built Fonograf out of our own need of a better way to discover music around us. Music tastes and events are an excellent opportunity to test users willingness to curate their own creativity in an open network. Band performances and their relationship with venues are an excellent example to test willingness for collaboration on stories. This led us to LENS™ and the need to give trusted sources, key contributors, and creators a direct posting platform to the most interested audiences.
