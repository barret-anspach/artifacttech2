Our Work

Every person has a story to tell, and so does every place. Whether it's a community, a local restaurant, a museum or any other point of interest, places in the real world are forced to tell their story online — disconnected from their location. Here at Artifact, we have bridged the gap between the real and the virtual with our LENS platform. LENS brings places alive by connecting the people who are present with digital information, entertainment and commerce.
