Cindi McCurdy

Senior Product Manager

Cynthia has over 20 years of experience creating products and managing teams for Microsoft, Tooned In Animations, Logitech, INRIX, Ford, AT&T, and Jamoki LLC. Cynthia’s work as a senior GPM/PM has produced an array of products from Excel, to Julia Child’s CD ROM “Home Cooking with Master Chefs”, to INRIX Inc.s’ cross-platform mobile Traffic application. She combines her early education in Film and Television with her degree in Computers & Business to design and deliver products using her technical project management, UI/UX skills.
