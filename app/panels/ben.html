Ben McAllister

VP, Development and Production

Ben McAllister has more than 14 years experience bridging tech­nology, science, and art developing several emerging technologies at key milestones in the music business including streaming audio, DRM, and emerging cross platform systems. As an interactive de­signer and installation artist, Ben's been featured in gallery work bringing his unique motion tracking and audio analysis software to visitors.

Two Favorite Hobbies:

Writing music and running

More on Ben:

AT: "Hey Ben, if you could spend one day at any job ever invented, what would it be and why?"

Ben: "On some huge Library of Congress grant, I'd collect field recordings of music and sound in some remote area, somewhere untouched by western civilization. I love recording new sounds, and I'd learn a lot about survival, sound and how humans interact with technology in the process."
